Tokyo

東京
Days 1 – 9
Day 1
Shimokitazawa
Tokyo — Alt Culture District
Key Activities
  • Vintage shopping & thrift stores
  • Indie cafes & live music houses
  • Record stores & indie anime shops

* No bookings needed. Check live house schedules day-of (tickets available same-day). Bring cash for small shops.

Day 2
Shibuya
Tokyo — Alt Culture Continued
Key Activities
  • Shibuya youth culture & street style
  • Live music venues & bars
  • Thrift shops & vinyl hunting

* Flexible day. Explore evening nightlife around Shibuya's backstreets.

Day 3
Nakameguro & Daikanyama
Tokyo — Chic Riverside
Key Activities
  • Meguro River canal walk
  • Boutique shopping & designer cafes
  • T-Site bookstore & galleries

* Best in afternoon/evening. Reserve ahead for popular cafes on weekends.

Day 4
Odaiba
Tokyo — Waterfront
Key Activities
  • Joypolis indoor theme park
  • Life-size Gundam Unicorn statue
  • Miraikan science museum & seaside park

* Buy Joypolis tickets online to skip the line. Gundam has scheduled light shows — check times in advance.

Day 5
Roppongi & Ebisu
Tokyo — Skyline & Dining
Key Activities
  • Mori Tower observation deck — panoramic city view
  • Roppongi art museums
  • Ebisu evening dining & drinks

* Book Mori Tower tickets online. Make dinner reservations — popular spots fill up fast.

Day 6
Ikebukuro
Tokyo — Anime & Pop Culture
Key Activities
  • Otome Road — manga & anime shopping
  • Pokemon Center Mega Tokyo
  • Arcades & game centers

* No booking needed. Go midday — Pokemon Center gets crowded in the evenings.

Day 7
Day Trip
Nikko
From Tokyo — Mountain Shrines
Key Activities
  • Toshogu Shrine & ornate carvings
  • Kegon Falls & mountain trails
  • Lake Chuzenji scenic views

* Leave early (7–8am). Consider the Nikko Pass for savings. Wear comfortable shoes — lots of walking.

Day 8
Day Trip
Kawagoe
From Tokyo — Little Edo
Key Activities
  • Edo-period warehouse district stroll
  • Street food & local snacks
  • Candy Alley (Kashiya Yokocho)

* Go morning — most shops close early (~5pm). No bookings needed.

Day 9
Day Trip
Yokohama
From Tokyo — Bayside City
Key Activities
  • Minato Mirai waterfront skyline
  • Cup Noodles Museum (make your own!)
  • Chinatown & Red Brick Warehouse

* Reserve Cup Noodles Museum slot in advance. Evening skyline is spectacular.

Hakone

箱根
Days 10 – 11
Day 10
Nature
Hakone
Mountain & Hot Springs
Key Activities
  • Hakone Ropeway & volcanic valley
  • Lake Ashi pirate ship cruise
  • Evening onsen (hot spring bath)

* Book ryokan/onsen early — critical for availability. Use luggage forwarding to avoid carrying bags. Check weather for Mt. Fuji views.

Day 11
Nature
Hakone
Art & Relaxation
Key Activities
  • Hakone Open-Air Museum
  • Morning onsen soak
  • Scenic gardens & forest walks

* Relaxed pace today. Transfer to Kyoto in the afternoon via shinkansen.

Kyoto

京都
Days 12 – 15
Day 12
Culture
Higashiyama
Kyoto — Historic East
Key Activities
  • Kiyomizu-dera temple & wooden terrace
  • Sannenzaka & Ninenzaka old streets
  • Traditional tea houses & cafes

* Go early morning to beat the crowds. No booking needed.

Day 13
Culture
North & East Kyoto
Kyoto — Zen & Gardens
Key Activities
  • Philosopher's Path canal walk
  • Nanzen-ji temple & aqueduct
  • Ginkaku-ji (Silver Pavilion)

* No booking needed. Rent a bike for this area — it's quieter and very pleasant.

Day 14
Culture
Fushimi Inari
Kyoto — Iconic Torii Gates
Key Activities
  • Thousand torii gate hike (2–3 hrs)
  • Mountain summit views
  • Fushimi sake brewery district

* Go at sunrise for the best experience (almost empty). The full hike is 2–3 hours. No booking needed.

Day 15
Day Trip
Uji
From Kyoto — Matcha Capital
Key Activities
  • Matcha tasting & tea ceremony
  • Byodo-in Phoenix Hall (UNESCO)
  • Nintendo Museum (if open — check lottery)

* Byodo-in ticket on arrival. Check Nintendo Museum lottery system in advance if applicable.

Osaka

大阪
Days 16 – 19
Day 16
Shinsekai & Tennoji
Osaka — Retro & Street Food
Key Activities
  • Retro neon streets & kushikatsu
  • Tsutenkaku Tower views
  • Tennoji Park & zoo area

* No booking needed. Tower ticket on-site. Best explored afternoon into evening when the neon lights up.

Day 17
Namba & Amerikamura
Osaka — Youth Culture & Shopping
Key Activities
  • Amerikamura vintage & streetwear
  • Dotonbori food walk
  • Cafes & people watching

* No booking needed. Flexible day — go with the flow.

Day 18
Nightlife
Namba & Shinsaibashi
Osaka — After Dark
Key Activities
  • Bar hopping & hidden speakeasies
  • Karaoke marathon
  • Late-night street food (yatai stalls)

* No booking needed. Bring ID (sometimes required). Last trains ~midnight — plan a taxi if staying out late.

Day 19
Day Trip
Kobe
From Osaka — Beef & Harbor
Key Activities
  • Legendary Kobe beef lunch
  • Kobe Harbor & Meriken Park
  • Nunobiki Herb Garden ropeway & Chinatown

* Reserve Kobe beef restaurant 2–7 days ahead — very important, top spots book out fast.

Hiroshima

広島
Days 20 – 21
Day 20
Culture
Hiroshima City
Hiroshima — Peace & Resilience
Key Activities
  • Peace Memorial Park & Museum
  • Hiroshima Castle grounds
  • Hiroshima-style okonomiyaki

* No booking needed. Allow 1–2 hours for the museum. Popular okonomiyaki spots may have a wait.

Day 21
Nature
Miyajima Island
Hiroshima — Sacred Island
Key Activities
  • Floating torii gate at Itsukushima Shrine
  • Mt. Misen hike or ropeway
  • Island street food & momiji manju

* Check ferry times. Go early to beat crowds. Ropeway has set operating hours — verify before going.
